What We Do

Salmon based-confections are a uniquely Pacific Northwest treat. Pat's original salmon cookies were born in the Pike Place market, through a happy accident: Pat was eating a pink-frosted cookie when one of the Pike Place Market fishmongers pegged her right in the face with a coho. After a year of intense development, Pat's Salmon Cookies was launched at our flagship location just a block away from where the idea was born.

Where we Are

Located in the heart of the historic Pike Place Market, our flagship cookie shoppe houses a cafe and cookie shop, as well as our test-recipe kitchen. Stop in for a hot cup of something dark and a delicious, locally sourced salmon cookie and watch our design team develop our next sensational salmon-confection!
