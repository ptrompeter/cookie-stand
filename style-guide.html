Fonts

Pat's Salmon Cookies uses font-family: 'Amatic SC', cursive for headers and highlights, font-family: ‘Palatino Linotype’, ‘Book Antiqua’, Palatino, serif for text and font-family: "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica, sans-serif for data.

Colors

The primary background color for Pat's Salmon Cookies is #FFC573. Black is the prinicple color of font on this background. Text boxes use #38293A as a background-color and #FE7933 for text. Data boxes use #9A3221 as a background-color and #FFC573 for content.
