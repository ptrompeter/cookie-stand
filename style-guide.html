Style guide

Logo

The following image is the basis of Pat's Salmon Cookies' Logo and will appear somewhere on each of Pat's pages. On the front page, it is a background image inserted into buttons on hover.

Fonts

Pat's Salmon Cookies uses font-family: 'Amatic SC', cursive for headers and highlights, font-family: ‘Palatino Linotype’, ‘Book Antiqua’, Palatino, serif, or 'Raleway' for text, font-family: "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica, sans-serif for data, and font-family: 'Great Vibes', cursive; for dividers, captions, and other elements requiring cursive flourish.

Colors

Pat's primary background color is #737590. Articles (primary text boxes) are #CAD6EF. The text on these boxes is black. Pat's header is black; the principle text on these boxes is #FECD2A, but secondary text is #E07BD5 and buttons are #63DEEE. At the moment the front page is going for a dated 1980's neon-nightlife kind of look inspired by a stolen photo of pike place market at night. Dividers are done in a wholly different style: color: #E79A0B on background-color: #CC0F29.

At the moment, Pat uses a wholly diffent style for internally facing pages, to distinguish them from public pages. The primary background color for internal pages is #FFC573. Black is the prinicple color of font on this background. Text boxes use #38293A as a background-color and #FE7933 for text. Data boxes use #9A3221 as a background-color and #FFC573 for content.

CSS for tables are still up for review.
